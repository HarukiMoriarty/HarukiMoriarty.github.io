Research Topic

My academic focus extends beyond the specificities of CloudOLTP, embracing a broader spectrum of Database Management Systems (DBMS). I am captivated by the challenges of scalable distributed transaction processing and their opportunities to enhance data management. The complexities of ensuring high availability, managing network latency, and achieving distributed commitment in these systems are the frontiers I am eager to explore. I aim to contribute significantly by innovating and implementing solutions that bridge the gap between theoretical frameworks and practical, real-world database applications.
